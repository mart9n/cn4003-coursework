Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Does the Web App work without internet connection?
  • A: The Web App works under the following circumstances:
    1. If you have internet connection.
    2. It will work in "stand-by" mode if you temporarily lose connection.
    3. If you do not have internet connection, you will not be able to terminate or submit new bike reservations
  • Q: What happens if I crash my bike?
  • A: You will not be liable to pay any damages if you:
    1. Call us within 30 minutes of the crash.
    2. In the case of a serious crash, you notify the authorities.
